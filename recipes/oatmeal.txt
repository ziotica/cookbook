[TITLE] Classic Oatmeal
[SERVINGS] 4
[COOK_TIME] 10 min 
[INGREDIENTS]
1 cup milk
2 cups rolled oats
2 tbsp Greek yoghurt
2 cups water
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup berries
12 almonds
[INGREDIENTS_LIST]
milk, oats, yoghurt, water, salt, berries, almonds, nuts
[METHOD]
1. In a medium saucepan over high heat bring milk, water, and salt to the boil. Stir in rolled oats, then reduce heat and simmer, uncovered, for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove the pan from heat. Cover and let stand for 2 minutes before serving.
2. Stir through the Greek yoghurt.
3. Top with nuts and berries.
