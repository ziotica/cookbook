[TITLE] Shakshuka
[SERVINGS] 4
[COOK_TIME] 15 min
[INGREDIENTS] 
3 cups (600g) cherry tomatoes
2 cloves garlic
1 onion
2 tsp za'atar
1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, grated
4 eggs
2 tbsp olive oil
1/2 tsp brown sugar
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper
1 long red chilli, finely chopped
Parsley leaves, to garnish
[INGREDIENTS_LIST]
tomato, garlic, onion, za'atar, cheese, egg, oil, sugar, salt, pepper, chilli, parsley
[METHOD]
1. Preheat oven to 190Â°C.
2. Finely chop the tomatoes, garlic and onion.
3. Heat the olive oil in a large ovenproof frying pan over medium heat. Add the sugar, salt and pepper and stir to combine. Cook for 30 seconds, then add the tomato micture and 1 teaspoon za'atar, continuing to stir to ensure everything is well mixed.
4. Allow the sauce to simmer for 5 minutes until thickened. Remove from the heat and sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the top. Crack eggs into the frying pan and then transfer it to the oven. Bake for 6 minutes. 
5. Remove from the oven and sprinkle with chopped chilli, if using, the remaining za'atar and parsley to garnish.
