[TITLE] Buckwheat Berry Pancakes
[SERVINGS] 2
[COOK_TIME] 15 min
[INGREDIENTS]
1 cup buckwheat flour
1 1/4 cups buttermilk
1/4 cup maple syrup
1/2 berries 
1 tbsp sugar
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
1 large egg
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
Butter
[INGREDIENTS_LIST]
buckwheat flour, buttermilk, maple syrup, berries, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, egg, vanilla extract, butter
[METHOD]
1. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
2. Place the buttermilk in a separate bowl, then add the egg and vanilla extract.
3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined - don't over-mix.
4. Place a medium frying pan over medium heat and allow it to get hot before adding a knob of butter. Pour around 1/4 cup of batter into the frying pan. Cook for 2-3 minutes until bubbles start to pop up on the surface. Flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden.
5. Repeat until all batter has been used up.
6. Serve with berries and maple syrup.
