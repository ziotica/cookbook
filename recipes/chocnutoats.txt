[TITLE] Chocolate and Nut Overnight Oats
[SERVINGS] 1
[COOK_TIME] 10 min + soaking
[INGREDIENTS] 
1 1/2 cups rolled oats
1/2 cup milk
2 tbsp peanut butter
1 tbsp cocoa powder
2 tsp honey
1 pinch cinnamon
[INGREDIENTS_LIST]
oats, milk, peanut butter, cocoa, honey, cinnamon
[METHOD]
1. In a glass or jar, thoroughly mix together the milk, peanut butter and cocoa. Then stir through the honey until combined.
2. Mix through the oats and cinnamon. 
3. Place in the fridge and let sit for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight.
4. Serve with toppings of choice.
[NOTES]
1. Possible additions: chia seeds, protein powder.
2. Oats can be eaten cold, or slightly warmed.
