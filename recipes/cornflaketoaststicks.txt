[TITLE] Cornflake French Toast Sticks
[SERVINGS] 4
[COOK_TIME] 15 min
[INGREDIENTS]
1/4 tsp cinnamon
2 cups (60g) cornflakes, crushed
4 slices of bread, cut into thick strips
2 eggs
2 tbsp milk
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
2 tbsp butter
Maple syrup
Blueberries
[INGREDIENTS_LIST]
cinnamon, cornflakes, bread, egg, milk, vanilla extract, butter, maple syrup, blueberries
[METHOD]
1. Please the eggs, milk, vanilla and cinnamon in a large bowl and whisk till combined.
2. Lay the crushed cornflakes onto a clean and dry work surface.
3. Dip each bread strip into the egg mixture and then roll it through the crushed cornflakes.
4. Melt the butter in a large frying pan over a medium heat.
5. Add crumbed bread strips to pan and cook for 2-3 minutes per side until crunchy.
6. Service with maple syrup and blueberries.
