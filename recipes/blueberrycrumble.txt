[TITLE] Blueberry Breakfast Crumble
[SERVINGS] 4
[COOK_TIME] 15 min
[INGREDIENTS]
4 cup mixed berries (500g), plus fresh blueberries for topping
2 tbsp cinnamon
1/2 cup oats
2 tbsp desicated coconut, plus extra for sprinkling
1 tbsp honey or maple syrup
2 tbsp walnuts, crushed
2 tbsp coconut oil, melted
Greek yoghurt
[INGREDIENTS_LIST]
berries, blueberries, cinnamon, oats, coconut, honey, maple syrup, walnuts, coconut oil, yoghurt
[METHOD]
1. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C.
2. Combine the berries and 1 tablespoon of cinnamon in a medium ovenproof dish. In a separate mixing bowl, combine the oats, coconut, honey, walnuts, coconut oil and the rest of the cinnamon and mix. Sprinkle the crumble mixture over the berries in the dish.
3. Cook in the oven for 10 minutes or until golden. Serve with fresh blueberries and yoghurt, sprinkling more desicated coconut over the top.
