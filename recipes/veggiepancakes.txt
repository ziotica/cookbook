[TITLE] Veggie Pancakes
[SERVINGS] 2 
[COOK_TIME] 15 min
[INGREDIENTS] 
1 1/2 cups (400g) cooked, mashed potato
2 spring onions, finely chopped
1/2 cup frozen peas 
1/2 cup frozen corn kernels
1/4 cup plain flour
2 eggs, lightly beaten
3/4 cup milk
Salt and pepper
Vegetable oil
[INGREDIENTS_LIST]
potato, spring onions, peas, corn, flour, eggs, milk, salt, pepper, oil
[METHOD]
1. In a large bowl, mix together the potato, flour, spring onions, peas, corn, eggs, and milk. Season the mixture with salt and pepper.
2. Shape the mixture into flattened patties and set aside.
3. In a large frying pan heat enough oil to sit at about 1cm deep.
4. Gently slide the patties into the oil and fry until crisp and golden all over, flipping once during cooking.
5. Place on paper towels to drain.
