[TITLE] Berry Crepes with Cherry Sauce
[SERVINGS] 4
[COOK_TIME] 15 min
[INGREDIENTS]
1 cup bottled sour cherry juice
1 1/2 cup cherries, pitted and halved
1 1/2 cup blueberries
1 1/2 cup milk
1 cup plain flour
2 eggs
30g butter, melted, plus extra for frying
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup sugar
1 tbsp cornflour
[INGREDIENTS_LIST]
cherry juice, cherries, blueberries, berries, milk, flour, egg, butter, salt, sugar, cornflour
[METHOD]
1. Preheat oven to 150Â°C and line a baking tray with baking paper.
2. Combine milk, flour, eggs, melted butter and salt in a food processor or blender and process until smooth. Transfer to a jug and set aside.
3. Combine sugar and cornflour in a saucepan. Add cherry juice and stir until smooth. Place over medium heat and cook, stirring constantly, until mixture begins to simmer. Add cherries, return to a simmer and cook for 2 minutes, stirring constantly. Set aside and keep warm.
4. Heat 1 teaspoon of butter in crepe pan over medium heat. Put in enough batter to coat the pan in a very thin layer, tilting pan to spread. Cook for 1 minute or until base is golden and top is set, then turn and cook for 1 minute or until golden. Transfer to tray and keep warm in oven.
5. Working one crepe at a time, top with a little warm cherry sauce, fold into quarters and transfer to a serving plate. Repeat with remaining crepes and sauce. Scatter with blueberries and serve hot.
