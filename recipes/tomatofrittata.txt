[TITLE] Frittata with Cherry Tomatoes
[SERVINGS] 2
[COOK_TIME] 10 min
[INGREDIENTS] 
3 egg whites 
2 eggs
6 cherry tomatoes
1 bunch fresh spinach (leaves picked)
1/2 cup (60g) Parmesan cheese, grated
1 1/2 tsp fresh basil, chopped
1/8 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/2 tbsp oil 
[INGREDIENTS_LIST]
egg white, egg, tomato, spinach, Parmesan, basil, salt, pepper, oil
[METHOD]
1. Preheat grill to medium setting.
2. Whisk egg whites and eggs together in a medium bowl and stir through whole tomatoes, spinach, cheese, basil, salt, and black pepper.
3. Heat oil in frying pan over medium heat. Add the mixture and cook, covered, for 3 minutes or until almost set. 
4. Remove from heat and place under grill for 2 minutes or until egg is set. Slice into 4 pieces and serve.
