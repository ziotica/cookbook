[TITLE] Eggs Baked in Avocado Boats
[SERVINGS] 2
[COOK_TIME] 15 min
[INGREDIENTS] 
1 large avocado, halved and pit removed
1/4 tsp ground cumin
1 tbsp red capsicum, finely chopped
1 spring onion, finely sliced
2 slices fresh lime
2 eggs
Salt and pepper
[INGREDIENTS_LIST] 
avocado, cumin, capsicum, spring onion, lime, egg, salt, pepper
[METHOD]
1. Preheat oven to 220Â°C.
2. Place avocado halves in separate ramekins (they must sit flat so the eggs don't run out). Sprinkle a pinch of cumin into each hole, then crack one egg into each avocado half. Season to taste.
3. Bake in the oven until egg is cooked to preference (or 12 minutes).
4. Remove from oven and sprinkle over the capsicum and spring onion. 
5. Squeeze over fresh lime to taste.
