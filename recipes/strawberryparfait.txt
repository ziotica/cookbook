[TITLE] Strawberry Parfait with Yoghurt and Granola
[SERVINGS] 4
[COOK_TIME] 15 min
[INGREDIENTS]
6 cups (1.2kg) strawberries
1 tsp cinnamon
1 lemon, zested and juiced
2 cups granola 
2 cups plain yoghurt
3/4 cup sugar
4 tsp pistachio nuts, chopped
Mint leaves, to garnish
[INGREDIENTS_LIST]
strawberries, cinnamon, lemon, granola, yoghurt, sugar, pistachio, mint
[METHOD]
1. Place 2 cups of strawberries, cinnamon, 1 teaspoon lemon zest, 2 tablespoons lemon juice and the sugar into a small saucepan and cook over a medium heat for 10 minutes, until strawberries are soft.
2. Transfer to a blender and puree on medium speed until smooth.
3. Prepare the remaining 4 cups of strawberries for the parfait by washing, hulling and slicing lengthwise.
4. Layer granola, strawberries, yoghurt and sauce in chilled glasses or jars. Garnish with sliced strawberries, pistachios and mint.
