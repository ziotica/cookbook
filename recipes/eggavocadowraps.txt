[TITLE] Fried Egg and Avocado Wraps
[SERVINGS] 4
[COOK_TIME] 10 min
[INGREDIENTS] 
4 large flour tortilla wraps
8 cherry tomatoes, halved
2 medium avocados, pitted and sliced
1 small red capsicum, seeded and finely chopped
4 large fresh eggs, room temperature
165g feta, crumbled
1 1/2 tbsp olive oil
Salt and pepper
1 cup coriander leaves
[INGREDIENTS_LIST]
tortilla, tomato, avocado, capsicum, egg, cheese, oil, salt, pepper, coriander
[METHOD]
1. Heat the olive oil in a large nonstick frying pan over medium-low heat.
2. Crack the eggs into the pan and reduce the heat to low. Fry for 4 minutes, then remove and place each egg on one of the wraps.
3. Add the tomatoes, avocado, capsicum, coriander and feta, as well as some salt and pepper. 
4. Serve the wraps open or carefully foll up and serve.
