[TITLE] Blueberry Chia Jam
[SERVINGS] 6
[COOK_TIME] 15 min
[INGREDIENTS]
1 1/4 cups (125g) fresh blueberries
1 tsp lemon juice
1 tbsp maple syrup
2 tbsps chia seeds
[INGREDIENTS_LIST]
blueberries, lemon, maple syrup, chia seed
[METHOD]
1. In a small saucepan, add 1 cup of the blueberries, lemon juice, and maple syrup and bring to a simmer or until the mixture begins to bubble and breakdown.
2. Remove the mixture from the heat and let it cool.
3. Using a blender, whiz the mix until reasonably well combined, then remove to a bowl.
4. Add the chia seeds and the rest of the blueberries and stir thoroughly. Make sure there are no chia seeds that have clumped together.
[NOTES]
1. While the jam can be used immediately, the mixture will thicken if left for an hour or two.
2. Store in an airtight glass container and it will keep for 1 week.
