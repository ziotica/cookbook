[TITLE] Tomato Omelette
[SERVINGS] 2
[COOK_TIME] 10 min
[INGREDIENTS]
6 eggs
1/2 red onion, chopped
3 Roma tomatoes, halved and sliced
1 cup (125g) cheddar cheese, grated
1/3 cup milk
125g butter
2 tbsp parsley, chopped
2 tbsp chives, chopped
[INGREDIENTS_LIST]
egg, red onion, tomato, cheddar, milk, butter, parsley, chives
[METHOD]
1. In a large jug, whisk eggs and milk together. Season to taste.
2. Melt half the butter in a frying pan over a medium heat. Add red onion and fry for 4-5 minutes, until soft, then add the tomatoes and stir for a few minutes until soft. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. 
3. Wipe out the pan with a paper towel. Melt the remaining butter in the same pan over a medium heat.
4. Add half of the egg mixture. When almost set, spoon on half the tomato mixture and add the cheese. Fold over and cook for 1 minute. Slide onto a serving plate.
5. Repeat with the remaining ingredients for the second omelette.
6. Sprinkle the omelettes with the parsley and chives, and serve.
