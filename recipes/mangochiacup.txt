[TITLE] Mango Chia Cups
[SERVINGS] 2
[COOK_TIME] 15 min
[INGREDIENTS]
1 cup coconut milk
3 tbsp chia seeds
2 tbsp honey or maple syrup
1/2 mango, pureed
4 tbsp coconut yoghurt
4 tbsp toasted museli
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
Fresh fruit or mint leaves, to garnish
[INGREDIENTS_LIST]
coconut milk, chia seeds, honey, maple syrup, mango, yoghurt, museli, vanilla extract, fruit, mint
[METHOD]
1. In a jar or mixing bowl, combine coconut milk, chia seeds, honey or syrup and vanilla.
2. Cover with plastic wrap and chill in fridge for 10 minutes to allow chia to absorb and swell.
3. Remove from fridge and layer half the mango puree, chia mixture, coconut yoghurt and museli in each serving glass.
4. Garnish with mint leaves and fruit of your choice. 
[NOTES]
1. For a firmer texture, or convenience, leave the chia in the fridge overnight.
