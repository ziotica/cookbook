[TITLE] Potato Rosti with Boiled Eggs
[SERVINGS] 4
[COOK_TIME] 15 min
[INGREDIENTS]
4 large eggs, room temperature
3 potatoes, grated and squeezed
1 large onion, grated and squeezed
1/2 tsbp fresh thyme, chopped
3 cups fresh rocket leaves
1/4 cup plain flour
50g butter, melted
Salt and pepper
Vegetable oil
[INGREDIENTS_LIST]
egg, potato, onion, thyme, rocket, flour, butter, salt, pepper, oil
[METHOD]
1. Place eggs in medium saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring to boil and reduce heat to a simmer for 7 minutes. Remove the eggs from the pot, peel, and set aside.
2. Mix together potato, onion, flour, thyme, butter, and salt and pepper.
3. Heat 1cm of oil in a large deep frying pan over medium heat. 
4. Add 1/3 cups of potato mix to the pan and flatten to 8cm rounds. Fry for 3 minutes a side until cooked and golden.
5. Served topped with rocket and egg on the side.
