[TITLE] Strawberry French Toast
[SERVINGS] 3
[COOK_TIME] 10 min
[INGREDIENTS]
3 eggs, lightly whisked
6 slices thick white bread
1/2 cup maple syrup
1 1/4 cups fresh strawberries
2/3 cup milk
1/4 cup caster sugar
60g butter
[INGREDIENTS_LIST]
egg, bread, maple syrip, strawberries, milk, sugar, butter
[METHOD]
1. Combine eggs, milk and sugar.
2. Heat a frying pan over high heat and melt butter until foaming.
3. Dip one slice of bread at a time into the egg and milk mixture, until soaked through.
4. Fry bread slice for 3 minutes on each side until golden brown. Repeat with remaining bread and mixture.
5. Serve hot with butter, maple syrup, and fresh berries.
