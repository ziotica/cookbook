[TITLE] Breakfast Hot Dogs
[SERVINGS] 4
[COOK_TIME] 15 min
[INGREDIENTS] 
4 sausages, cooked
4 flour tortilla wraps
2 cups (60g) mixed salad leaves
1 cucumber, sliced
1 carrot, grated
[INGREDIENTS_LIST]
sausage, tortilla, salad, cucumber, carrot
[METHOD]
1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C.
2. Warm the tortillas according to packet instructions.
3. Lay out each tortilla on a flat surface and place a sausage in the middle. Add salad, cucumber and carrot to the tortillas and fold.
