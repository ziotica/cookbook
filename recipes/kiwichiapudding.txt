[TITLE] Kiwi Chia Pudding
[SERVINGS] 4
[COOK_TIME] 15 min
[INGREDIENTS]
4 tbsp chia seeds
1 1/2 cups coconut milk
2 kiwi fruits, peeled and quartered
1 banana (frozen)
1/4 cup coconut water
1 tsp vanilla extract
Chia seed and flaked almonds, for topping
[INGREDIENTS_LIST]
chia seeds, coconut milk, kiwi fruit, banana, coconut water, vanilla extract, almonds
[METHOD]
1. Place the chia seeds in a small bowl. Pour over the vanilla and coconut milk and stir. Set aside to soak for 10 minutes.
2. Place the kiwi fruit, banana and coconut water in a blender and process until smooth enough to drink.
3. Transfer the chia mixture to serving glasses, then top with the kiwi mixture, and top with extra chia seeds and almonds to serve.
