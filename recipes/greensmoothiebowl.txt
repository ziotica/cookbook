[TITLE] Green Smoothie Bowl
[SERVINGS] 4
[COOK_TIME] 5 min
[INGREDIENTS]
2 cups almond milk
2 cups (60g) baby spinach
1 kiwi fruit, sliced
1 zucchini, frozen
4 kale leaves (de-stemmed)
Mixed nuts, chopped
1 tbsp honey
[INGREDIENTS_LIST]
almond milk, spinach, kiwi fruit, zucchini, kale, nuts, honey
[METHOD]
1. Place milk, spinach, zucchini and kale in a blender unti all the ingredients are combined.
2. Pour the mixture into serving bowls or cups. Add kiwi fruit slices on top and sprinkle with nuts.
