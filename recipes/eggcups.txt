[TITLE] Egg Cups with Ham and Tomato
[SERVINGS] 2
[COOK_TIME] 15 min
[INGREDIENTS] 
4 slices ham 
2 Roma tomatoes (sliced)
4 large eggs
Oil
Salt and pepper
[INGREDIENTS_LIST]
ham, tomato, egg, oil, salt, pepper
[METHOD] 
1. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C.
2. Lightly grease four muffin trays. Press a slice of ham into each muffin cup. Crack an egg into each cup and arrange tomato slices on top.
3. Bake for 10 minutes on the middle shelf of the oven, until whites are cooked but yolks are still runny.
4. Season eggs with salt and pepper and remove from muffin trays.
