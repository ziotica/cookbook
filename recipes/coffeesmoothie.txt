[TITLE] Morning Coffee Smoothie
[SERVINGS] 1
[COOK_TIME] 5 min
[INGREDIENTS]
1 tsp instant coffee or 1/2 cup brewed coffee, chilled
1 tsp cocoa powder
1 tsp coconut sugar (or honey)
1 cup coconut milk
1/4 tsp cinnamon (plus more to garnish)
1 cup ice cubes
[INGREDIENTS_LIST]
coffee, cocoa, coconut sugar, honey, coconut milk, cinnamon, ice
[METHOD]
1. Add all the ingredients to a blender and process until smooth enough to drink. Add more milk if required.
2. Pour into a tall glass, garnish with cinnamon and serve.
[NOTES]
1. This can also be make with cow's milk.
2. For a creamy top, add frothed milk or whipped cream.
